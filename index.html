Welcome to Buea BloodLink, Donate Blood, Save Lifes in Buea.

Buea Blood link connects voluntary blood donors with hospitals in need, making it faster and easier to respond to emergencies and save lives.

How It Works

  1. Register as a donor or hospital.
  2. Create your profile with your blood type and location or hospital location and bloodtype and unit needed
  3. Search for available blood types or post a request.
  4. Request blood or book a donation appointment.
  5. Respond to request and help save lives.
